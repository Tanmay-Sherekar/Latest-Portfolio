About Me

I am a passionate and results-driven Full Stack Web Developer with strong hands-on experience in PHP, WordPress, frontend technologies, and a growing specialization in DevOps and Cloud Engineering. Over the past few years, I have worked across multiple roles including junior PHP developer, software developer, WordPress developer, and freelance engineer, which has helped me build a solid foundation in both application development and deployment practices. I enjoy working at the intersection of development and operations, where code meets infrastructure, automation, and performance optimization.

My professional journey started with a strong interest in web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. As I progressed, I moved deeper into backend development using Core PHP and MySQL, building dynamic, data-driven applications. I have worked extensively with WordPress, not just as a CMS user but as a developer who understands theme customization, plugin integration, performance optimization, security hardening, and server-side configurations. This experience allowed me to deliver real-world projects for businesses, healthcare platforms, and service-based websites, ensuring scalability, responsiveness, and maintainability.

While working on PHP and WordPress projects, I developed a strong understanding of how production systems behave in real environments. This naturally led me toward DevOps and cloud technologies. I realized that writing good code is only one part of building reliable systems—deploying, monitoring, scaling, and securing applications is equally important. This realization motivated me to upskill myself in Linux administration, Docker, CI/CD pipelines, and AWS cloud services.

I have hands-on experience working with Linux-based servers, where I manage application deployments, configure web servers, troubleshoot performance issues, and maintain system stability. I actively use Docker to containerize PHP and WordPress applications, ensuring consistency across development, testing, and production environments. By creating Dockerized setups with Nginx, PHP-FPM, and MySQL, I am able to reduce deployment issues and improve development efficiency.

In the DevOps space, I have worked with CI/CD tools such as GitHub Actions and Jenkins to automate build, test, and deployment workflows. I believe automation is a key pillar of modern software delivery, and I continuously look for ways to eliminate manual steps, reduce errors, and speed up release cycles. My pipelines focus on code quality, repeatability, and reliability, helping teams deliver updates faster and with greater confidence.

On the cloud side, I have practical experience with AWS services such as EC2, IAM, and basic networking concepts. I understand how to deploy applications on cloud infrastructure, manage access securely, and optimize resources for performance and cost efficiency. I am actively expanding my cloud knowledge to include advanced AWS services, infrastructure best practices, and container orchestration concepts like Kubernetes, with the goal of becoming a well-rounded DevOps engineer.

What sets me apart is my ability to understand the full lifecycle of a web application—from designing user-friendly interfaces and writing backend logic to deploying applications on cloud servers and automating workflows. My background as a developer helps me collaborate effectively with development teams, while my DevOps skills allow me to think proactively about scalability, security, and operational stability.

I have worked in contractual roles, internships, and freelance projects, which has taught me adaptability, accountability, and effective communication. These experiences helped me understand real client requirements, tight deadlines, and the importance of delivering quality work consistently. I am comfortable working independently as well as collaborating within a team environment, following best practices and continuously learning from feedback.

I strongly believe in continuous learning and self-improvement. Technology evolves rapidly, and I make it a point to stay updated with industry trends, best practices, and emerging tools. Whether it’s improving my PHP coding standards, learning new DevOps tools, or understanding cloud cost optimization strategies, I approach learning with curiosity and discipline.

Beyond technical skills, I value clarity, problem-solving, and reliability. I enjoy breaking down complex problems into manageable solutions and building systems that are not only functional but also efficient and maintainable. I take pride in writing clean, readable code and setting up systems that others can easily understand and extend.

Looking ahead, my goal is to grow as a Full Stack Developer with a strong DevOps mindset or transition fully into a DevOps/Cloud Engineer role. I am particularly interested in working on scalable systems, cloud-native applications, and automated infrastructure. I am open to full-time roles, contract opportunities, and challenging projects where I can contribute meaningfully while continuing to grow technically.

If you are looking for someone who understands both development and deployment, values automation, and is committed to building stable, scalable solutions, I would be excited to connect and explore opportunities to work together.
